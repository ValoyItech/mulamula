Community Facilities

Essential services and facilities that serve our community and surrounding areas

🚔

Mulamula Satellite Police Station

Location: Magangeni (Mbhayimbhayi) village
Service Area: 18 villages in Malamulele West region
Services: Community policing, crime prevention, emergency response

Note: One of the few satellite police stations serving rural communities in the region

📚

Community Library

Status: One of few libraries in Collins Chabane Local Municipality
Planned Location: Dumela village (as per official documents)
Services: Educational resources, community programs, literacy support

Historical Note: Decision made by late Hosi Mulamula VI in April 2019

🏛️

Mulamula Tribal Office

Location: Dumela village
Services: Traditional governance, administrative services
Additional Services: Home Affairs & Social Development (partial use of old building)

Function: Central hub for traditional authority administration and government services

Community Library Development Timeline

Initial Planning Phase

Library project initiated as part of community development plans

Location debate: Magangeni vs Bulwini village

April 2019 - Official Decision

Hosi Mulamula VI, late Mdungazi Joseph Maluleke-Photani, submitted official wishes to former MEC Ms Thandi Moraka

Decision: Convert disputed building to circuit office, build library at Dumela village

Official Documentation

All supporting documents confirmed Dumela village location

Includes: application, approval, tender advert, and SOPA by former Premier

Current Status

Community library serving educational needs of all four villages

One of the few libraries in Collins Chabane Local Municipality

By Ernest Mabasa | May 6, 2025

Limpopo Premier Dr. Phophi Ramathuba has been accused of making fruitless promises to settle the R23 million Dumela Library Project. Local residents in Collins Chabane Municipality are questioning whether the Mulamula Library is built in the right village, as it sits 7km away from the intended Mulamula-Dumela village location.

Business Compliance Requirements

  • Royal Permission: Businesses must get approval from the Traditional Authority
  • Community Consultation: Plans discussed with village leadership
  • Cultural Respect: Operations aligned with traditional values
  • Regular Reporting: Maintain communication with the Authority
